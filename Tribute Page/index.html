It's not that we need new ideas, but we need to stop having old ideas. — Edwin H. Land

A picture means I know where I was every minute. That’s why I take pictures. It’s a visual diary. — Andy Warhol

The man is a national treasure. I don’t understand why people like that can’t be held up as models: This is the most incredible thing to be — not an astronaut, not a football player — but this. — Steve Jobs

Timeline

  • 1909 — Born in Bridgeport, CT
  • 1929 — Land files first patent for first synthetic polarizer at 20 years old.
  • 1937 — Company renamed to Polaroid Corporation and listed on New York Pacific Stock Exchange (PRD)
  • 1943 — Edwin Land first concieves the instant camera
  • 1948 — Model 95 Land Camera goes on sale at Jordan Marsh
  • 1956 — 1 millionth Polaroid camera rolls off the assembly line
  • 1962 — Ansel Adams - Yosemite National Park images - Type 55 negative to gelatin silver. Mr. Adams was a consultant from 1949.
  • 1971 — Big Shot camera is release and Andy Warhol immediately embraces it.
  • 1972 — SX-70 introduced. Within a year 5000 of these cameras are produced per day.
  • 1973 — Walker Evans - last works on Polaroid
  • 1973 — Resigned as Presidential Advisor to Nixon administration. #197 member of Nixon's "200 enemies" list.
  • 1973 — Lucas Samaras: First to manipulate the dyes of an SX-70 image in 1973
  • 1979 — Chuck Close: Several Photographs stitched together.
  • 1981 — Muppets: Miss Piggy's gets Sun 600 for her Birthday and Fozzie gets one for Christmas
  • 1982 — David Hockney works with Polaroid film
  • 1991 — Death (Mount Auburn Cemetery in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA)
  • 2002 — Polaroid stock stops trading.
  • 2008 — The Impossible Project is founded in the Netherlands.
  • 2008 — Impossible Film starts selling film.
  • 2009 — Polaroid film is discontinued.
  • 2014 — The Polaroid Museum opens in Las Vegas, Nevada
  • 2016 — Impossible starts manufacturing its own instant camera - Impossible I-A.
  • 2017 — Impossible Film's largest shareholder acquires the brand and intellectual property rights to the original Polaroid Corporation
  • 2017 — Impossible Film is renamed Polaroid Originals
  • 2019 — Polaroid Week started in 2006. 2019 is the week of Apr 21.
